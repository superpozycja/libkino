home about submissions

submissions to libkino.com are curated by hand right now. dm @superpozycja if you have a suggestion. she's very open to most submissions.

accepted submission types:

  • books
  • websites
  • articles
  • videos
  • software (when justified)
this site does not list people by themselves, so don't submit your twitter/github profile/personal website.

authorship of the submitted material is acknowledged, however, self- submissions are not allowed. if you're good enough to be here, someone will probably submit your work anyway.

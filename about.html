libkino.com is a hub of knowledge and inspiration for computer scientists and hackers around the world.

there's a lack of a central aggregate of links, articles, books, and other media that can be considered valuable for the curious mind. such materials are exchanged all the time on social media, however, due to the fleeting nature of such platforms, it's easy to get lost if one does not keep a repository of found resources. libkino.com attempts to be such a repository, while simultaneously being public, which increases exposure for people who might not otherwise have stumbled upon a particular piece of media through another channel.

libkino.com is made and curated but @superpozycja.
